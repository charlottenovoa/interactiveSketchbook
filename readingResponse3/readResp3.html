Reading Response #3

Which rule feels most absent from your micro-project page?

n my opinion, my site violates the eight rule. All of the navigation is not consistent and is not all on the same page.

Which rule would most improve your sketchbook interface?

Rules eight and one would probably assist my site the most as one would improve my visuals (usability wise) and eight would improve my layout.

Which rule is hardest to implement honestly?

Five is the hardest in my opinion. It is difficult to anticipate other’s errors because I can’t know everything they do. It’s hard to see those issues because I know how to navigate everything, but someone who isn’t me may run into problems I haven’t seen.

Did any rule contradict how you designed your system originally?

I think the rule about consistency wasn’t really something I considered when I first put my project together. I had just wanted to get everything working in any way, and fit it all somehow.
