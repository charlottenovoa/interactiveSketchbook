#3: Busy

I wanted this sketch to feel a bit overwhelming because a busy morning can feel like you have a lot going on. The different shapes and colors can kind of represent how we have to keep track of so many things when planning our day and things can look a little messy. I played around with the shapes moving together with, and at odds with the mouse to add to the messyness.
