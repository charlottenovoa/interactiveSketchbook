Ideas for Micro Project

Idea One

Waiting

Sketch with keyPressed or mousePressed style input, but the sketch can't stop "loading." Has the delay function in between these inputs. These delayed sections will show the feeling of waiting.

Idea Two

Gaslighting

Every so often the input doesn't work and the screen prompts the user with something like "are you sure you clicked?" or "try again, no click detected." On a bar trying to be completed.

Idea Three

Overwhelming

Alternates between different scenarios. For example, trying to complete a shape, drawing a letter, etc. Constantly changes to show how multitasking can be overwhelming.
