#1: Frustrating

This sketch features a square that moves away from the mouse. When things don't go your way it can be a bit frustrating. The rectangle has a threshold where it moves away if the mouse is in that range. I chose red because I thought it was just the color of anger and frustration, and green is a good contrast to that.
